# 🤝 SolarPunk Mutual Aid
## A needs board, skills board, resource pool, and emergency fund. No bureaucracy. No applications. No gatekeeping.

---

> Mutual aid is not charity. Charity flows downward — from those who have, to those who don't, on the giver's terms.
> Mutual aid flows in all directions simultaneously. Everyone gives. Everyone receives. The network is stronger than any node in it.

---

## Four Boards

**NEEDS BOARD** — Post what you need. No shame. No means test. No application.

**SKILLS BOARD** — Post what you can give. Time, knowledge, tools, access.

**RESOURCE POOL** — Things that are abundant get shared. Bandwidth, storage, compute, seeds, tools, space.

**EMERGENCY FUND** — Rapid response. Real crises get real help, fast, with no paperwork.

---

## How to Post

Open a GitHub Issue. Use the template. Done. The community sees it. People respond directly. No middleman holds the request.

Templates in `/issues/`:
- `need.md` — what you need, when, any relevant context
- `offer.md` — what you can give, to whom, under what conditions
- `resource.md` — what's available to share
- `emergency.md` — urgent needs, flagged for fast response

---

## The Philosophy

This system has no approval process because approval processes are how gatekeepers stay gatekeepers.

The only rule: be honest about what you need and what you have. The network is smart enough to handle the rest.

---

## Connect This to Your Community

Fork this repo. Open it to your neighborhood, city, Discord, co-op, housing collective, mutual aid pod — whoever. The Issues board becomes your community's board. The Emergency Fund links to your community's payment pool. The mycelium spreads.
